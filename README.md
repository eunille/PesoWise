# PesoWise

## Your Personal Savings Advisor

PesoWise is a modern platform that helps Filipino savers understand their financial growth potential. Compare multiple savings options side-by-side and watch your money grow with interactive projections.

---

## Why PesoWise?

With so many savings products available in the Philippines, it's hard to know which one is right for you. PesoWise makes it simple:

✓ **Compare Multiple Banks** - See how your money would grow at GoTyme, Maya, GSave, MariBank, and PDIC side-by-side

✓ **No Sign-Up Required** - Start comparing in seconds with zero friction

✓ **Visual Projections** - Watch your savings grow month-by-month with interactive charts

✓ **Transparent Calculations** - All projections are based on actual current interest rates

✓ **Mobile-Friendly** - Access from your phone, tablet, or computer anytime, anywhere

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is PesoWise free?**
A: Yes, completely free. We don't charge any fees, ever.

**Q: Do you take a commission from banks?**
A: No. PesoWise is a non-profit comparison tool. We make no money from banks or users.

**Q: How accurate are the projections?**
A: Very accurate. We use each bank's actual current interest rates and standard compound interest formulas.

**Q: Will my data be sold?**
A: We don't collect personal data, so there's nothing to sell. Ever.

**Q: Can I save my calculations?**
A: Not yet, but you can take a screenshot or write down the results. Future versions may support saved calculations.

**Q: Why is PDIC included with only 0.5% interest?**
A: PDIC is the safest savings option for amounts up to ₱500,000. Some savers prioritize safety over returns.

## The PesoWise Story

We created PesoWise because we were frustrated. With dozens of savings products and different interest rates at different banks, there was no easy way to compare options. 

We wanted a simple, transparent tool that showed the real impact of interest rate differences. Something you could use in 2 minutes to compare your real savings potential.

The result? PesoWise - your personal savings advisor.
